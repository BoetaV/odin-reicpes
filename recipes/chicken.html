Chicken Marsala

Prepare with ease

I’ve always loved the versatility of chicken recipes and this one doesn’t disappoint one bit. This is the type of dish you usually get at a restaurant, but it’s super simple to make right at home.

  • Chicken breast fillets breaded
  • in a bit of flour
  • pan fried to a golden perfection
  • and served with a mushroom and Marsala wine sauce

This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.
